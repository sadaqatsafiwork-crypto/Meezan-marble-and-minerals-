About Us

Pakistan's Finest Natural Stone Factory

Located in Subhan Khwar, District Charsadda, KPK — Meezan Marble and Minerals is a direct-source factory delivering premium natural stone to projects across Pakistan. Every slab is hand-selected from mountain quarries for exceptional quality, veining, and character.

  • Direct factory pricing — no middlemen, maximum value
  • All types: marble, granite, tiles, stairs, kitchen tops & flooring
  • Custom cutting and sizing available on order
  • Supplying residential, commercial, and architectural projects
  • Fast dispatch from KPK to all Pakistan cities
